About Me!

Born in Florida, Ryan moved to Tennessee for his parents work as a young child.

Ryan joined the military at age 17 and shipped out to basic in June of 2011 he became a Medic in the army in December of 2011.

From there he worked in the 1st Cavalry Division based out of Fort Hood Texas.
While Working with only soldiers for the 2 year duration he was ready in 2014 to challenge himself so he changed positions to an Emergency Room technician in Fort Belvoir Va
This is where he worked with soldiers and their families solving health problems and trauma with military doctors.
Ryan left the military in 2016 to pursue his other passion in tech.
He attended Cabrillo college in Santa Cruz CA for 3 years when he discovered Make School and realized he could be working in the field at a much faster capacity than if he were to attend traditional college..
