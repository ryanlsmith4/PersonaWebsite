About Me!

Born in Florida, Ryan moved to Tennessee for his parents work as a young child.

Ryan joined the military at age 17 and shipped out to basic in June of 2011 he became a Medic in the army in December of 2011.

From there he worked in the 1st Cavalry Division based out of Fort Hood Texas. Working with only soldiers for 2 years when in 2014 he changed positions to an Emergency Room technician in Fort Belvoir Va, where he worked with soldiers and their families solving health problems and trauma with military doctors.
Ryan left the military in 2016 to pursue his other passion in tech. He attended Cabrillo college in Santa Cruz Ca, for 3 years when he discovered the Make School and realized his passion could be realized sooner in a less traditional school. Now Ryan is Studying Computer Science and working towards his degree.
